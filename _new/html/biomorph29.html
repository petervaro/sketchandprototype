Organic shape experimentation — a digital design process study.

After the digital sketches, the shape is modeled in a mesh modeling system, for fast feedbacks and easyier modifications.

Once the initial mesh is done, it is remodeled as a NURBS surface.

Then the CAD model is split into smaller assemblable parts for easier manufacturing.
